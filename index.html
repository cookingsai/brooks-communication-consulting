Services

Every person's communication journey is unique. I offer a range of services tailored to meet you where you are.

🗣️

Speech Therapy

Evidence-based speech-language pathology services for children and adults. Addressing articulation, fluency, voice, and language disorders in a supportive, individualized setting.

💼

Consulting

Professional communication consulting for organizations, schools, and healthcare providers. Expert guidance on communication strategies, program development, and team training.

🌍

English for Non-Native Speakers

Specialized coaching to improve spoken English clarity, pronunciation, and professional communication for non-native English speakers — in the workplace and everyday life.

About

Meet Carla Brooks

I'm a licensed speech-language pathologist with a passion for helping people communicate more effectively. Whether you're working through a speech or language challenge, navigating a new language, or looking to enhance your professional communication skills, I'm here to help.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and evidence-based. I believe every person deserves to be heard and understood — and that the right support can open doors you never thought possible.

I provide services to clients of all ages and backgrounds, both in-person and virtually.
